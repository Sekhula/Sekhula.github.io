Greetings ladies & gentlemens, my name is Forgiveness, and I'm a full stack developer.

More About Me

My first employment as a developer was at The Digital Academy, in December 2021 as an intern for IT Systems support. Through incentive-based learning with strategic corporate partners, the Digital Academy internship program aims to provide opportunities for the youth.


I gained significant soft skills while working on intriguing projects. My team and I worked on an application called Edu-App for our final project, which allows grade 12 learners to engage with other learners from different school across the country, it also allows them to book registered teachers across the country in case they need extra one-on-one lesson.


Users can utilise this application with either mobile phones or any other computer. I got to participate in all elements of the project with my team, including UI and UX design, database design, frontend programming, backend development, and deployment.
